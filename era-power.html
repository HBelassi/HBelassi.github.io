ERA-POWER

UXR / Industrial Design

PHOENIX Design Studio
Industrial Designer
2 days sprint

ERA-POWER is an electric vehicle charging station custom-designed to be part of the urban furniture of Egypt's New Administrative Capital — a symbol of sustainable progress rooted in cultural heritage.

Add your project image here

Background.

Had the pleasure of spending 2 productive days at PHOENIX design studio working on this task. In an ambitious bid to embrace a sustainable future, the city is embarking on a groundbreaking initiative to integrate EV charging stations seamlessly into its urban landscape.

Phase I: Pick a target city, outline reasons for selection, provide a quick cultural and geographic brief, research global trends in EV charging stations, conduct a competitor audit.

Phase II: Design should reflect brand-typical elements — consumer-oriented, high usability and functionality, independent appearance while maintaining brand identity.

New Administrative Capital.

An ambitious project initiated in 2015 and still under construction, the New Administrative Capital stands as a pivotal component of Egypt's Vision 2030. The New Capital embodies a harmonious blend of history and technology — aiming to transform Egypt's global image while honoring its rich cultural legacy.

The Ancient Egyptian Lotus.

The Lotus flower, revered for its representation of purity and rebirth, held significant symbolism in ancient Egyptian culture. This symbolic choice serves as a powerful bridge between Egypt's illustrious history and its current technological ambitions — making it the ideal design inspiration for ERA-POWER.

Ideation.

The art deco design movement, influenced by ancient Egyptian aesthetics like the lotus, emerged as a natural design language — blending geometric precision with organic forms, creating a charging station that feels both futuristic and deeply rooted in cultural identity.
