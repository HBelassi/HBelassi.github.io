Slim.VU

Problem Solving / Industrial Design

Personal Project
Industrial Designer
Design Sprint

Slim.VU, a highly integrated portable smart projector, redefines on-the-go entertainment with its built-in battery and seamless connectivity. Slim.VU seamlessly connects users to the product through innovative design, providing a unique audio-visual experience wherever they go.

Add your project image here

Background.

Projectors often pose common issues such as their unwieldy size, tangled wires, and an abundance of accessories. Slim.VU was conceived to solve all of these in a single, elegant form.

Ideation.

The focal point in refining concepts centered around elevating the overall experience provided by a portable projector. Diverse forms were explored through 3D printing, evaluating promising ideas against criteria like intuitive use, ergonomics, and visual impact.

Personal Theater At Your Disposal.

Slim.VU brings a cinematic experience to any space — a hotel room, a backyard, or a living room wall.

Seamless Connectivity.

USB-C connector that offers audio, video transmission, power charging, and high data transfer speed simultaneously — a single cable that does it all.

Smart Companion.

Slim.VU is powered by the Android TV platform, delivering an uninterrupted streaming experience — all integrated, no external devices needed.

Validating.

To achieve best results in terms of functionality, usability, and aesthetics, extensive 3D printing of form explorations was conducted — testing proportions, grip, and projection quality across various environments.
