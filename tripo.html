TRIPO

Problem Solving / Industrial Design

Personal Project
Industrial Designer
Design Sprint

Meet TRIPO, the ultimate portable theater. Serves as a smart projector and a premium wireless speaker in one sleek device. Immerse yourself in a world of entertainment, whether it be your cherished shows, movies, or live streams.

Background.

The concept of TRIPO emerged from personal experience with home projection. Key challenges identified: adjusting height and angle, restriction to cables, limited mobility, and bulky sizes.

Ideation.

TRIPO's distinctive design empowers it to effortlessly pivot across four different angles, all without any tools or adjustments — solving the angle and positioning challenge in a single gesture.

Add your project image here

Heat Dissipation.

Circular air vents on both sides enhance airflow and cooling without compromising the sleek design aesthetic.

Stability.

The body's form widens as the base broadens, ensuring stability on any surface without the need for a separate tripod.

Seamless Connectivity.

USB-C connector that offers audio and video transmission, power charging, and high data transfer speed simultaneously.

Precision Imagery.

TRIPO supports automatic Multi-Angle Keystone Correction along with AutoFocus that sharpens the image automatically — ensuring a crisp picture at any projection angle.

Smart Companion.

TRIPO is powered by the Google Android TV platform, delivering an uninterrupted streaming experience — all integrated. TRIPO also doubles as a speaker: connect wirelessly via Bluetooth to enjoy your favorite tracks with premium audio quality.
