Hey, I'm Hana.

(Currently based in Haarlem, The Netherlands)

A bit about myself, I'm a multidisciplinary problem solver with expertise in UX research, data analytics and industrial design. My passion lies in creating intuitive and user-centered digital experiences by blending strategic design, innovative thinking, and data-driven insights.

With experience across industries like automotive, home appliances, and strategic design, I've led teams to solve complex challenges by conducting in-depth user research and data analysis. By leveraging tools like Excel and Dovetail, I have gathered actionable insights through A/B testing, surveys, and usability studies, which directly informed design decisions and product optimization. My analytical approach ensures that every decision is backed by data, leading to measurable improvements in product performance and user satisfaction.

In addition to my research and design expertise, I regularly collaborate with cross-functional teams to align UX strategies with business goals. I've developed user personas, user journeys, and optimized back-end operations, all while ensuring scalability and seamless system functionality.

With the growing influence of AI tools, I've enhanced my design process further by integrating advanced technologies, making my work more efficient and impactful.

Whether I'm diving deep into data to uncover user pain points or developing innovative design solutions, I'm always looking for new challenges where I can make a tangible impact by blending creativity with analytical rigor.
