XGIMI
Industrial Designer / 3D Visualizer
3 weeks

XGIMI is a pioneering technology company specializing in advanced projection systems, revolutionizing home entertainment. XGIMI approached me to design an April Fool's Day product to boost brand awareness and social media engagement.

Background.

The brief: create a versatile portable projector concept featuring an integrated liquid bottle/coffee tumbler — a deliberately playful, fictitious product designed to generate buzz.

Ideation.

Despite being a fictitious product, I approached it with authenticity, mirroring a genuine design process. Following initial sketching and rapid brainstorming, recurring patterns prompted four distinct design directions:

Illuminate: Light beam travels through liquid to create an interesting experience. Projector lens at the bottom with full diameter. Flip-open legs acting as pivoting tripod.

Horizontal Split: 50/50 split — half transparent liquid container, half metal cylinder housing the projector. Stanley cup-inspired drinking mouth with silicon hand band for grip.

Fading Frost: Fading transparency from frosted opaque bottom to transparent top, creating the illusion of liquid floating in air. Fading speaker pattern as well.

Vertical Split: Splitting the form vertically with a 1:3 ratio. Sports spout drinking cap. Separated bottle from projector for cleaning purposes. Mimicking XGIMI's brand identity.

Chosen Direction.

Following internal discussions, the XGIMI team collectively chose the Vertical Split concept as the most compelling and brand-consistent direction. Refined details included polished buttons and logo for a luxurious feel, sloped edges around the legs for better tripod activation, and variable speaker pattern intensity (100% / 50% / 25%).

Final Outcome and Visualization.

For the final deliverable, the XGIMI team requested three rendered posters: one in a studio setting, one in a lifestyle/outdoor context, and one detail close-up. The product was shared as a social media giveaway post that generated significant engagement.
