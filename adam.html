ADAM

Industrial Design

SUDOTECHS
Industrial Designer
Design Sprint

ADAM is a personal assistant robot designed to enhance business meetings. It operates using TREMVO software — a peer-to-peer recognition platform made to appreciate, reward, and motivate teams in real time.

Add your project image here

Background.

ADAM had to be always present in all business meetings, thus it was designed to imitate the formal role of a meeting attendee. The goal was to blend a professional "Suit & Tie" appearance with the presence of a miniature robot that commands attention without intimidating.

Ideation.

Several design iterations underwent company-wide surveys to achieve the most fitting and aesthetically balanced form — one that felt professional, approachable, and distinctly memorable.

Add your project image here

Meet ADAM.

ADAM's most iconic design feature is the interactive illuminated section resembling a tie — serving as both a visual identity element and a functional display for TREMVO notifications and recognition moments.

ADAM seamlessly integrates with a variety of applications and software, all governed by TREMVO's core recognition engine. It listens, records, and attributes — automatically recognizing contributions and rewarding team members during meetings.
